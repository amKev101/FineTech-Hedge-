HOW IT WORKS

Follow these five steps:


1. Create your strategy.

Rule-based system which allows you develop different types of strategies: CTA, risk-party, factor models, etc.


2. Analyze your strategy.

Review 100+ potential issues to make sure that the strategy is robust before going live.
(Patent Pending)


3. Improve your strategy.

Our detailed reports help understanding the behavior of the strategy from different angles and provide recommendations on how to mitigate several issues.


4. Combine strategies into a portfolio.

Rubust portfolios are built by combining several uncorrelated strategies.


5. Receive real-time signals to trade.

You can receive them via email, FTP, or APIs, and can be connected to your Broker's Order Management System software.
